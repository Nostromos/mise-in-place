Ingredients
2 chicken thigh
2 potato
1 white onion
4 cloves garlic
Â½ tbsp Olive oil
1/4 tsp Sea salt
1/4 tsp Pepper
1 tsp Spanish paprika
1 tbsp Herbs d'provence


Directions
Heat oven to 425.

Peel potato(s), remove black/brown spots, and slice. Leave skin on.

Heat up skillet and add some oil.

Halve and slice onion(s).

Add potatoes and onions to skillet and let them start to brown. Salt and pepper.

Peel garlic cloves and add to skillet.

Prep chicken thigh. Add salt, Spanish paprika, and herb d'province.

Let cook for 2 minutes.

Stick whole skillet into oven for 25-30 minutes.
